WELCOME TO

Hotel  Joylife,
      Rohtak

Hotel Joylife, a 3 Star hotel in Rohtak, is also one of the best luxury hotels in the city and is conveniently located near the Rohtak Bus Station and Railway Station. Hotel Joylife has stylish rooms as well as a restaurant. Every stay is enhanced by luxurious fabrics and stylish furniture.
Some nearby attractions in the district include Durga Bhawan Mandir, Gurdwara Bangla Sahib, Tilyar Lake, Bhindawas Lake, and Mahem. The natural beauty and calmness of the district are so relaxing that it is possible to spend a vacation here.
